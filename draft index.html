About FFDC

Built on four pillars.
Delivered through community.

Faith & Family Development Center is a faith-integrated training and coaching organization committed to community transformation. Everything we do is organized around a Four Pillars Model — equipping people to break destructive patterns and build lives of purpose, connection, and stability.

Personal Development

Identity, purpose, self-awareness, and the patterns that shape every other area of life.

Healthy Relationships

Communication, boundaries, conflict resolution, and the foundations of secure connection.

Leadership

Servant leadership, emotional intelligence, legacy, and influence in family and community.

Family Stability

Parenting, household systems, generational healing, and the building blocks of a stable home.

Three Facilitator Tiers

There's a place for you,
wherever you're coming from.

Whether you carry a clinical license, a pastoral calling, or a coaching credential — FFDC has a tier designed around what you bring.

Tier 1

Core Faculty

LPCs · LMFTs · LCSWs

  • Lead clinical curriculum tracks
  • Anchor the program's clinical credibility
  • In-person & virtual cohorts
  • Revenue share compensation
Tier 2

Ministry Faculty

Ordained Ministers · Pastoral Counselors

  • Faith integration across tracks
  • Marriage & family track leadership
  • Church partnership programming
  • Per-session or revenue share
Tier 3

Practitioner Coaches

ICF-Certified Coaches · Mentors

  • 1-on-1 coaching delivery
  • Cohort accountability support
  • Virtual & flexible scheduling
  • Per-session flat fee
