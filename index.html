About FFDC

Built on four pillars. Delivered through community.

Everything FFDC does is organized around the Four Pillars Model — equipping people to break destructive patterns and build lives of purpose, connection, and stability.

Personal Development

Identity, purpose, and the patterns that shape every area of life.

Healthy Relationships

Communication, boundaries, and the foundations of secure connection.

Leadership

Servant leadership, emotional intelligence, and lasting legacy.

Family Stability

Parenting, household systems, and generational healing.

Three Facilitator Tiers

There's a place for you, wherever you're coming from.

Tier 1 — Core Faculty

LPCs · LMFTs · LCSWs

  • Lead clinical tracks
  • In-person & virtual cohorts
  • Revenue share comp

Tier 2 — Ministry Faculty

Ordained Ministers · Pastoral Counselors

  • Faith integration
  • Church partnerships
  • Per-session or rev share

Tier 3 — Coaches

ICF-Certified Coaches · Mentors

  • 1-on-1 coaching
  • Virtual & flexible
  • Per-session flat fee
